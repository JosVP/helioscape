The Combined Venus + Mars Decision Space
What's interesting is that these choices interact across planets. A player who chose Spun Venus (redirected Europa) no longer has Europa available for Mirror Mars's water delivery — they have to use comet redirection or subsurface drilling instead. Titan nitrogen used for Mars means Titan can't be terraformed later. The choices aren't just about individual planets — they're about what kind of solar system you're building, and what you're willing to spend to get there.
